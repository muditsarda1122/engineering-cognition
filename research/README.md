# Research Notes

This directory documents the ongoing development of Engineering Cognition.

Unlike the paper, which presents a coherent snapshot of the current hypothesis, these notes capture the evolution of the research itself.

They include:

- how the idea emerged,
- questions that remain unanswered,
- assumptions currently being made,
- terminology used throughout the project,
- future research directions,
- and experimental planning.

These notes are expected to evolve continuously as the research matures.
