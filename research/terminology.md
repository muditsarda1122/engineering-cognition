# Terminology

## Engineering Cognition

The continuously evolving internal representation of a software system that enables future engineering decisions to improve through accumulated understanding rather than repeated reasoning.

---

## Observation

Information generated during software development.

Examples include:

- architectural discoveries
- debugging insights
- implementation decisions

---

## Cognition Candidate

An observation proposed for becoming persistent engineering cognition.

---

## Canonical Cognition

Engineering cognition that has been reviewed and accepted.

---

## Historical Cognition

Previously valid cognition retained for historical reasoning.

---

## Engineering Memory System (EMS)

One experimental implementation exploring Engineering Cognition.

EMS should not be interpreted as synonymous with Engineering Cognition itself.
