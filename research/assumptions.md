
# Current Assumptions

The following assumptions currently guide this research.

They are hypotheses rather than established facts.

## Assumption 1

Software engineering is cumulative.

Every engineering task contributes to future engineering decisions.

---

## Assumption 2

Current coding agents largely behave as episodic systems.

Reasoning accumulates within a session but is not reliably preserved across sessions.

---

## Assumption 3

Engineering understanding is distinct from conversations, source code, and commit history.

---

## Assumption 4

Engineering cognition can potentially improve long-horizon software development.

This remains to be experimentally validated.

---

These assumptions should be continuously challenged through future experiments.
